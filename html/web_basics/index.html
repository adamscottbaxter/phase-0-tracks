Baby Info

Babies really are a life changer just like you have heard many other people say before. They bring both times of great happiness as well as moments of stress. Here are some things you need to have set up before having a baby:

  • A crib
  • Bottles
  • Diapers
  • Baby clothes

Here is a list of milestones to look out for:

  1. Smiling
  2. Reaching for things
  3. Sitting
  4. Turing out pretty cool

To learn more about parenting visit r/parenting.
